Core Ideas
Sign Chart Method Find the zeros. They divide the number line into intervals. Test one value per interval — the sign at that point holds for the whole interval.
Quick Pattern (opens up, roots r₁ < r₂) $f(x)<0$: between roots $(r_1, r_2)$.   $f(x)>0$: outside roots $(-\infty,r_1)\cup(r_2,\infty)$. Flip both for opens-down ($a<0$).
