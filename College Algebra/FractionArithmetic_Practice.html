Fraction Arithmetic

Foundations Review  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson

Core Ideas
Add / Subtract Get a common denominator first, then add or subtract the numerators. Always reduce the result.
Multiply / Divide Multiply straight across (numerator × numerator, denominator × denominator). To divide, multiply by the reciprocal of the second fraction.
Practice Problems

Enter your answer as a reduced fraction like 3/4, or a whole number if it simplifies to one. Press Check or hit Enter.
