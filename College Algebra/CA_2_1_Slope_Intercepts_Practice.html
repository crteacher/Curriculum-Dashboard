Slope and Intercepts

College Algebra  ·  §2.1  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Slope Formula $m = \dfrac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}$   (rise over run). Positive: rising. Negative: falling. Zero: horizontal. Undefined: vertical.
Intercepts y-intercept: set $x=0$ and solve.   x-intercept: set $y=0$ and solve.
Practice Problems

For intercepts, enter an ordered pair: (0,5) or (3,0). For slopes, enter an integer. For slope-type: positive, negative, zero, or undefined. Press Check or hit Enter.
