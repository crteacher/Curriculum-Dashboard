Increasing, Decreasing, Positive & Negative

College Algebra  ·  §3.4  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Positive / Negative $f$ is positive where $f(x)>0$ (graph above x-axis). $f$ is negative where $f(x)<0$ (graph below x-axis). Boundaries are x-intercepts — find them by setting $f(x)=0$.
Increasing / Decreasing Increasing: graph rises left to right (positive slope). Decreasing: graph falls left to right (negative slope). Express the answer as an x-interval.
