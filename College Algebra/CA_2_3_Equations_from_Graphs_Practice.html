Equations from Graphs

College Algebra  ·  §2.3  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Reading the y-intercept If the line visibly crosses the y-axis at an integer, read $b$ directly. Then pick another point to compute $m = \text{rise}/\text{run}$.
Two-Point Method Compute $m$ from two points, then use point-slope: $y - y_1 = m(x-x_1)$ and simplify to $y = mx + b$.
Special Lines Horizontal through $(a,k)$: $y = k$.   Vertical through $(h,b)$: $x = h$.
Practice Problems

Graph problems: read the value directly from the grid and enter an integer. Text problems: most answers are integers; yes or no for true/false. Press Check or hit Enter.
