Further Counting Techniques

College Algebra  ·  §S.9  ·  Instructor: Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Exactly k Heads in n Flips Choose which $k$ of the $n$ slots are heads: $C(n,k)$.   Order of selection doesn't matter — only which slots are chosen.
At Least k — Complement "At least $k$" = Total $-$ "fewer than $k$".   Total outcomes for $n$ flips $= 2^n$.   Often faster than summing many cases directly.
Lattice Paths Walking $r$ steps right and $d$ steps down (no backtracking): choose which $r$ of the $r+d$ steps go right.   Count $= C(r+d,\,r)$.
