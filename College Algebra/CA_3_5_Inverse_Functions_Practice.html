Core Ideas
One-to-One & Horizontal Line Test A function is one-to-one if every output comes from a unique input. Graphical check: every horizontal line crosses the graph at most once. Only one-to-one functions have inverses.
Finding f − ¹ Replace $f(x)$ with $y$, swap $x$ and $y$, solve for $y$. Key property: $f^{-1}(f(x))=x$ and $f(f^{-1}(x))=x$. The graphs of $f$ and $f^{-1}$ are reflections over $y=x$.
