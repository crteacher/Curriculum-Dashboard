Triangle Definition of Trig Functions

Pre-Calculus  ·  §5.3.5  ·  Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026  ·  STUDENT

If someone says $\sin 37°=0.6$, what does that have to do with the sides of a right triangle? How are the unit circle and right-triangle definitions connected?
SOH-CAH-TOA

For a right triangle with angle $\theta$, hypotenuse $h$, opposite side $o$, adjacent side $a$:

$$\sin\theta=\dfrac{o}{h},\quad \cos\theta=\dfrac{a}{h},\quad \tan\theta=\dfrac{o}{a}$$

$$\csc\theta=\dfrac{h}{o},\quad \sec\theta=\dfrac{h}{a},\quad \cot\theta=\dfrac{a}{o}$$

Mnemonic: SOH-CAH-TOA.

Special Triangles

45-45-90: sides $1,1,\sqrt2$.
$\sin45°=\cos45°=\dfrac{\sqrt2}{2}$; $\tan45°=1$.

30-60-90: sides $1,\sqrt3,2$.
$\sin30°=\dfrac{1}{2}$, $\cos30°=\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}$, $\tan30°=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt3}$.
$\sin60°=\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}$, $\cos60°=\dfrac{1}{2}$, $\tan60°=\sqrt3$.

Worked Examples
Example 1 — Find trig ratios from a triangle
A right triangle has legs 3 and 4. Find all six trig functions for the angle opposite the side of length 3.
Hyp $=5$. $\sin=\tfrac{3}{5}$, $\cos=\tfrac{4}{5}$, $\tan=\tfrac{3}{4}$, $\csc=\tfrac{5}{3}$, $\sec=\tfrac{5}{4}$, $\cot=\tfrac{4}{3}$.
Example 2 — Use special triangles
Find $\sin 45°+\cos 30°$.
$\dfrac{\sqrt2}{2}+\dfrac{\sqrt3}{2}=\dfrac{\sqrt2+\sqrt3}{2}$.
Example 3 — Find a missing side
A right triangle has hypotenuse 10 and one angle $\theta=35°$. Find the opposite side.
$o=10\sin35°\approx10(0.574)=5.74$.
Example 4 — Find the angle
A right triangle has opposite 5 and adjacent 12. Find angle $\theta$.
$\tan\theta=\dfrac{5}{12}\Rightarrow\theta=\arctan\dfrac{5}{12}\approx22.6°$.
Practice Problems
1.
In a right triangle with legs 5 and 12, find $\sin\theta$ where $\theta$ is opposite the side of length 5.
$\dfrac{5}{13}$.
2.
Find $\tan60°$ using the special triangle.
$\sqrt{3}$.
3.
Find the adjacent side if $\cos\theta=\dfrac{3}{5}$ and hyp $=15$.
$a=9$.
4.
Find $\sin30°+\tan45°$.
$\dfrac{1}{2}+1=\dfrac{3}{2}$.
5.
A ladder 20 ft long leans at $70°$ to the ground. How high up the wall does it reach?
$20\sin70°\approx18.8$ ft.
6.
Find angle $\theta$ if $\sin\theta=0.5$ ($0°\leq\theta\leq90°$).
$\theta=30°$.
