Transformations of Functions

PreCalc  ·  §1.5  ·  Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Transformation reference
Form Effect Notes
$y=f(x-h)$ Shift right $h$ units Inside the function → horizontal, opposite sign
$y=f(x+h)$ Shift left $h$ units Adding inside → moves left
$y=f(x)+k$ Shift up $k$ units Outside the function → vertical
$y=f(x)-k$ Shift down $k$ units Subtracting outside → moves down
$y=-f(x)$ Reflect over $x$-axis Negates all outputs
$y=f(-x)$ Reflect over $y$-axis Negates all inputs
$y=af(x),\; a>1$ Vertical stretch by $a$ Multiplies all $y$-values by $a$
$y=af(x),\; 0<a<1$ Vertical shrink by $a$ Compresses toward $x$-axis
