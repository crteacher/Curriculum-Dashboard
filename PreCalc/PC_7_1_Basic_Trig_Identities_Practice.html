Basic Trig Identities

PreCalc  ·  §7.1  ·  Christopher Halverson  ·  Fall 2026

Core Ideas
Pythagorean identities $\sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta=1$
Divide by $\cos^2\theta$: $\tan^2\theta+1=\sec^2\theta$
Divide by $\sin^2\theta$: $\cot^2\theta+1=\csc^2\theta$
Even / odd identities $\cos(-\theta)=\cos\theta$   (cosine is even)
$\sin(-\theta)=-\sin\theta$   (sine is odd)
$\tan(-\theta)=-\tan\theta$   (tangent is odd)
