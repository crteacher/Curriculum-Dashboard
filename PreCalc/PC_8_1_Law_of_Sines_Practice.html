Core Ideas
Law of Sines For any triangle with angles $A,B,C$ and opposite sides $a,b,c$:
$\dfrac{a}{\sin A}=\dfrac{b}{\sin B}=\dfrac{c}{\sin C}$
The largest side is always opposite the largest angle.
